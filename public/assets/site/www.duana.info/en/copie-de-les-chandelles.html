    The pendulum
     

    A pendulum is a dowsing tool. It has nothing to do with spiritualism. In fact, it is an energy sensor which allows us to obtain answers. Before using it, you should define, for yourself, the direction of rotation which will give the answer yes, no, maybe and dont know.

    To do this, it is enough, from the first use and after purification, with water, coarse salt or incense, to calm down and sit quietly and ask, pendulum in hand: What is my yes? Whats my no? And so on Write it down on paper and keep this information as acquired forever. Your pendulum will always respond the same way.
     

    A pendulum can be made of wood, metal or stone. For its shape, it must be flat on top; this is its receiving side of energies and, pointing downwards; this is its transmitter side. A pendulum therefore very often has the shape of an inverted cone.
     

    What is dowsing?
     

    Dowsing is a divinatory process for detecting energies emitted by:

    • the different subtle bodies of the human being or an animal

    • a living organism (plants for example)

    • an object having been borrowed from an energy of its owner, former or current

    • a place
       

    Dowsing allows you to find a lost object, make a diagnosis (except medical which is reserved for doctors), answer one or more questions depending on a situation and a question asked. It remains a process;esoteric because it reveals what is hidden and invisible. It is widely used in clairvoyance, energy and some alternative medicines.
